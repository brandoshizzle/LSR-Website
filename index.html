  • What We Do

    Releasing music can feel daunting. But it doesn't have to be. Our team of musicians, producers, designers, software developers, and promoters will make sure your release is successful. This includes:

    • Production, mixing, and mastering
    • Access to our library of VSTs & nature sounds
    • Artwork design and artist branding
    • Distrubution to all major streaming platforms
    • Placement on LSR playlists and pitching to our list of curators
    • 24/7 artist support and access to our Discord server where you can get feedback from our community and discuss collaborations

  • Our Philosophy

    The music industry has evolved. Big Label no longer holds the power.

    We, the artists do. Our vision is to help you turn your passion into a viable career path. While the most important, writing a beautiful song is just one piece of the puzzle. We’ve taken a particular interest in understanding what makes a release successful. By applying our learnings, our artists have amassed over 100 million streams on Spotify, and we’re constantly seeking out new ways to make each release a success.


    It’s no secret that we are a business. But that doesn’t mean we have to prioritize profit. Our goal is to help you succeed. We’re a label, but that doesn’t mean we have to behave like one. We both share equally in revenue generated. Everything you create is yours. We don’t own your music. No exclusivity clause bullshit. Come and go as you please. Most importantly - if you feel like we aren’t holding up our end of the bargain, let us know and we’ll release 100% of future revenue to you… no questions asked.
